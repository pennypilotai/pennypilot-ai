PennyPilot AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Introduction

PennyPilot AI is an offline personal finance application designed to help users track and manage their financial information while maintaining privacy.

Information We Collect

PennyPilot AI does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All financial records, notes, and app data remain stored locally on the user's device.

Data Storage and Security

Your financial information is stored only on your device. PennyPilot AI does not use cloud storage, external databases, advertising networks, or third-party analytics services.

Permissions

The application may request device permissions required for providing app functionality. These permissions are used only for the operation of the application.

Third-Party Services

PennyPilot AI does not share user data with third parties. The application does not include advertising services or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

PennyPilot AI does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
